Cybersecurity Tips

Strong Passwords

Use strong passwords with numbers, symbols and uppercase letters.

Use passwords longer than 8 characters.

Avoid Suspicious Links

Do not click suspicious links from unknown sources.

Phishing links steal personal information.

Verify Messages

Always verify messages asking for money or personal data.

Scammers impersonate banks or friends.

Two-Factor Authentication

Enable two-factor authentication for important accounts.

2FA adds an extra layer of security.

Update Devices

Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches.

Updates fix security vulnerabilities.

Secure WiFi

Avoid using public WiFi for sensitive transactions.

Use VPN or trusted networks only.
